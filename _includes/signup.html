Uncle Goose Wants YOU!

Regardless of skill or experience everyone is welcome to join the GoOSe Project!.

.

Joining the project is a simple matter of following a few simple steps:

  • Join the GoOSe Project mailing list.
  • Subscribe at https://groups.google.com/group/goose-linux
  • Introduce Yourself!
  • Email an introduction to goose-linux@googlegroups.com
  • Update the CONTRIB.rst file.
  • Fork a branch of https://github.com/gooseproject/join
  • Add your information to the RST file.
  • Submit a pull request.

      • Welcome Aboard!
